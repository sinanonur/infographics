COVID-19 Impact

The pandemic has accelerated automation and AI adoption, particularly in areas with high physical proximity requirements.

Key Challenge: Workforce Shift

Rather than mass unemployment, the primary challenge is helping workers transition to different roles as AI takes over certain tasks.

MGI

Skills in Demand & Recommendations

Skills in Demand

  • Social skills
  • Creative thinking
  • Analytical capabilities
  • Technological proficiency
  • Adaptability and resilience
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Emotional intelligence

McKinsey's Recommendations

  • Focus on retraining programs
  • Invest in education systems
  • Create workforce transition support
  • Develop lifelong learning opportunities
  • Public-private partnerships for skills development
  • Strengthen social safety nets during transitions

AI will create demand for social, creative, and analytical skills that are harder to automate

Key Takeaways

  • AI will transform job markets with both job creation and elimination
  • Net effect expected to be a 2% reduction in current employment by 2027
  • 75% of companies plan to adopt AI, with half expecting job growth
  • AI-related roles are among the fastest-growing occupations
  • Administrative and clerical roles face highest risk of automation
  • Focus should be on workforce transitions rather than fearing mass unemployment
  • Skills in social interaction, creativity, and analysis will be increasingly valuable
